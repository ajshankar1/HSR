About Hotel Sriram

Four and a half decades of hospitality at the heart of Tirunelveli.

Our story

Built on the trust of returning guests since 1977

Hotel Sriram was founded by Sri Umasankar in 1977, opposite what was then a smaller bus terminus on the edge of Tirunelveli. Over the decades the hotel grew alongside the city, adding rooms, an in-house restaurant, and later the Vivahaa and Aishwaryam event halls.

Today the hotel is still family-run, with many staff who have worked here for over a decade. Guests range from pilgrims visiting the region's temples to wedding parties and business travellers passing through the junction.

The same principle has carried the hotel since day one: a clean, comfortable room and a genuinely warm welcome, at a fair price.
