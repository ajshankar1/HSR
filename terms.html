Terms & conditions

Please read the policies below before booking a room or banquet hall at Hotel Sriram.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Reservations & check-in

All bookings made through this website, by phone, or via WhatsApp are treated as requests and are confirmed only once our front desk has verbally or in writing confirmed availability and rate. A booking is not guaranteed until confirmed by hotel staff.

  • Check-in time: 12:00 PM. Check-out time: 11:00 AM.
  • Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability and may attract an additional charge.
  • A valid government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar, passport, voter ID, or driving licence) is mandatory for every adult guest at check-in.
  • Unmarried couples and local residents may be asked for additional verification at the hotel's discretion.

2. Rates, payment & GST

Room and banquet hall rates shown on this website are indicative estimates and may vary by season, occupancy, and final negotiated terms. All rates are subject to applicable GST as per Government of India regulations in force at the time of stay or event.

  • Room tariffs up to ₹1,000 per night: GST exempt.
  • Room tariffs between ₹1,001 and ₹7,500 per night: 5% GST applies.
  • Room tariffs above ₹7,500 per night: 18% GST applies.
  • Banquet hall bookings, catering, and event services: 18% GST applies regardless of value.
  • Payment is accepted by cash, UPI, or card at the hotel. Online prepayment is not currently processed through this website.
  • An advance payment may be requested to confirm banquet hall bookings during peak wedding season.
GST rates are determined by current Government of India regulations and may change without notice. The figures shown in the on-site calculators are estimates for guest reference only and do not constitute a final invoice.

3. Cancellation & refund policy

Room bookings:

  • Free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in.
  • Cancellations within 24 hours of check-in, or no-shows, may be charged one night's tariff.

Banquet hall bookings:

  • A booking advance, once paid, is non-refundable if cancelled within 30 days of the event date.
  • Cancellations made more than 30 days before the event date are eligible for a partial refund of the advance, less any costs already incurred (decor, catering procurement, etc.).
  • Date changes are accommodated where possible, subject to hall availability.

4. Guest conduct & hotel policies

  • Smoking is permitted only in designated areas.
  • Pets are not permitted in guest rooms or banquet halls unless prior written approval has been given.
  • Outside catering is not permitted in banquet halls without prior approval; the hotel's in-house catering team handles all food and beverage service.
  • The hotel reserves the right to refuse service or check-in to any guest behaving in a manner that is unsafe, abusive, or disruptive to other guests or staff.
  • Guests are responsible for any damage caused to hotel property during their stay or event, and may be charged accordingly.

5. Liability

Hotel Sriram takes reasonable care of guests' belongings but is not liable for loss, theft, or damage to personal property unless caused by proven negligence on the part of hotel staff. Guests are advised to use in-room safes where provided and not to leave valuables unattended.

6. Website & online enquiries

This website's booking and enquiry forms are request forms, not a live payment or instant-confirmation booking engine. Submitting a form does not guarantee a reservation. All figures shown by the room and banquet hall calculators (including GST) are estimates for planning purposes; the final invoice issued at the hotel is the only binding document.

7. Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws of India and fall under the jurisdiction of the courts in Tirunelveli, Tamil Nadu.
